The [Shill Cares Foundation](https://www.shillcares.org/) focuses on supporting animal shelters and human welfare organizations in the Rochester, NY area. It was founded by Molly and Ed Shill, who have a long history of supporting local causes. The foundation provides grants, donations, and support to organizations that help animals and people in need.

![Shill Cares Homepage](/projects/shill-cares/screenshot-1.jpg)

Applicants seeking grants can apply through the website. The new website design features a clean, modern layout that highlights the foundation's work and mission. It includes information about the founders, the organizations they support, and how to get involved.
